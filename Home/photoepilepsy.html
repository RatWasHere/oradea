Photosensitive Seizure Warning
A small percentage of people may experience seizures or loss of consciousness when exposed to certain visual images, flashing lights, patterns, or visual effects.
Even people who have no history of epilepsy or seizures may have an undiagnosed condition that can cause these symptoms.
If you or anyone in your family has a history of seizures or epilepsy, consult a physician before playing.
Immediately stop playing and consult a doctor if you experience dizziness, altered vision, eye or muscle twitching, loss of awareness, disorientation, involuntary movements, or convulsions.
This game contains flashing lights and visual patterns that may trigger seizures in susceptible individuals.
